About me

The name “Nagi” comes from “Nagisa”, which means “Beach” and “Coast” in Japanese. I started using it 7 years ago because of a cartoon I watched as a teenager and I got used to being called that. As I live on an island and almost studied Oceanography, the name seems even more suitable. Haha

My real name is João Pedro (John Peter, in english) and I'm 20 years old. I am studying Economic Sciences, but I already studied a degree in Mathematics which I abandoned in favor of economics. I've discovered a certain appreciation for programming in the meantime, so I have plans to pursue a graduate degree in Data Science when I finish college.

I am a Buddhist, following mainly the Zen Buddhism's perspective and in particular the Soto Shu School. I like reading A Doutrina de Buda, which I think is great introductory material.

I'm currently taking a javascript fullstack programming course on Udemy and getting ready to start researching to write my monograph, which I should present in the first half of 2025.
